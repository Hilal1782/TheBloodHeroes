Ashraf Ali

General Secretary

I joined this wonderful team named 'The Blood Heroes' as a Twitter handler and Records Team member. It never occured to me that I'd be working with this team actively in the long run.

My experience in TBH is ull of ups and downs. I've been with TBH in all sorts of good and bad, I have experienced the worst days as well the days in which TBH was praised on National Level, achieving milestone after milestone.

I am currently proud to lead and represent a team that has proven to be the pivotal point in the time of crisis known to be called covid, a team that provided a big number of donors/blood to the patients in need. My Provincial team has also remained the most active one even on-ground activities (150+) just this year; for which I want to thank every member who contributes their best for this good cause without expecting anything in return.
