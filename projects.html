OUR PROJECTS

The Blod Heroes is a student-based nationwide blood society whose main goal is voluntarily providing blood to the needy, promoting voluntary blood donations, and spreading awareness about blood-related issues. We organize projects like The Bleed Green, where we arrange blood donation volunteers registration, awareness events, blood donation camps and visits thalassemia centers to meet and encourage thalassemia kids.

Blood Camp Peshawar

September 24, 2020, Peshawar team held a small scale blood camp of 15+ total pints donated. An informative demonstration was also conducted in collaboration with Hamza Foundation, Where Mr. Mian Attique, Mr. Attique and Dr. Tariq showed the team the workings of the foundation, its contributions and achievements.

The team spent a memorable day with the children of the Foundation. The Camp was led by our Directors, Dr. Gulalai Arshad and Syed Shakeel Badshah, Deputy Director, Sumayyah Saqib, KPK representative, Syeda Ramsha Hassan and Peshawar representative, Ashraf Ali. The organizing team of the camp constituted of Talha Shinwari, Zaryab, Shoaib, Ashraf and other members.

Inshallah we will continue such camps to arrange blood donors and to create awareness and to promote prevention of thalassemia.We thank our donors for taking out the time and coming to donate.

Help Us Grow Our Blood Donation Camps by volunteering with us and by supporting us!

International Thalassemia Day

Spreading awareness about thalassemia among the masses is one of TBH's goals.

Team The Blood Heroes organized an awareness event in collaboration with PPA KP and LR Pediatric in accordance with “International Thalassemia Day 22.” The main aim of the event was to spread thalassemia awareness among the masses. It was an immense pleasure seeing over 100 participants on ground while 1900+ people joined us through Facebook Live.

TBH appreciated the efforts of volunteers working on this cause and raised thalassemia awareness through the event. We are honored to share that we have established a Thalassemia registry for KP and arranged for weekly MDT aimed at Thalassemia Care in LR.

A huge thank you to everyone who supported us!
